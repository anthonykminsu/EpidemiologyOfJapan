COVID-19 Case Count within U.S. Counties (among 18 and older)
January 2024
Map Information

Description: This map attempts to show the total number of COVID-19 cases among individuals aged 18 and older within U.S. counties. The size of the circles displayed are determined on the total count of cases within each county. Each county is represented by a dot which makes it easier for the user to locate there desired county. The data was collected in 2020 through the U.S. Census Bureau.

Users: Aspiring epidemologist, geography students, data scienctists

Data Sources: The data was collected from The U.S. Census Bureau
