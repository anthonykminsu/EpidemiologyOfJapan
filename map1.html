COVID-19 Case Rate in the United States (among 18 and older)
January 2024
Map Information

Description: Recorded in 2020, this map shows the COVID-19 case rate among individuals aged 18 and older within U.S. counties. The rate is calculated by taking the total number of cases within that country, dividing it by the population count, and then finally multiplying the value by 1000 in order to get the displayed rate. Above we have the spatially displayed rates that are recorded based on the statistics of the given county.

Users: UW Geography students, aspiring geographers, and avid map-makers

Data Sources: The data was collected from The New York Times and the U.S. Census Bureau
